Product Range

Rainbow Reading is designed for students who are reading below expected levels. Selections is for older students (14 years plus) who are reading at lower reading levels. The Toxic and Totally Toxic series appeals to reluctant readers.

Choose A Path books (also available as ibooks) present readers with lots of reading opportunities. Speak Out Reader’s Theatre (available in hard copy and digital format) is for all students working in groups of mixed ability.

All resources are available with audio support (CDs, mp3 file and reading pen) and activities.

Proven Effectiveness

Research consistently demonstrates that students (including English language learners) make, on average, gains of two years’ progress in reading level after using the Rainbow Reading Programme for just 16 weeks. The programme also improves students’ enjoyment, confidence, and competence in reading.

Export Success

Rainbow Reading is widely used in Australia, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom, through exclusive distributorships. It is available from Brightpoint Literacy in the United States as the New Heights Programme.

Business and Partnership Opportunities

For all resources and titles listed we are offering distribution, co-edition, translation, licensing and re-versioning rights worldwide except in the United Kingdom, Southern Ireland, Northern and Central Europe, Egypt and Australia.
