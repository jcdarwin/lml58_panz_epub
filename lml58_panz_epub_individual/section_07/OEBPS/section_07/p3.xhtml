New Zealand is at the forefront of indigenous education, with a curriculum designed specifically for Māori.

Hana Limited works with the New Zealand government to produce innovative, culturally relevant curriculum resources for Māori learners and teachers.

We believe that great resources should reflect a learner’s environment, culture and aspirations. They should empower children and inspire a love of learning.

We have developed award winning educational resources that support New Zealand’s world leading curriculum.

Services

Hana Limited offers services that include consultation, concept development, design, writing, editing, illustration, audio and digital media development (including for television), print management, production and distribution.

Our expertise lies in working with people to share stories and create meaningful, culturally appropriate educational products that resonate with learners, teachers, and their communities.
