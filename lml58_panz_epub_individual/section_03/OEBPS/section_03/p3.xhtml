Product Range

Clean Slate Press produces quality resources for students aged 4–14.

Highlights include:

Early Literacy: The Joy Cowley Club – Ages 4–7, Guided Reading Levels 2–17

Currently contains 90 titles with 60 titles in development, all written by early literacy expert Joy Cowley. Supported by audio CDs and teacher’s notes, Joy’s carefully levelled texts sing off the page”. Engaging Big Books and ebooks for iPad are available for many titles.

Middle Literacy: Signatures – Ages 8–12, Guided Reading Levels 20–30

A series of 60 chapter books. Joy Cowley has written 15 titles and edited 45 more. Guided Reading lesson plans and Blackline Masters available.

Inquiry Learning: Wise Up! – Ages 7–10, Guided Reading Levels 21–25

An Inquiry Learning approach to teaching literacy and maths content, including fiction and non-fiction titles. Inquiry Learning teacher’s notes outlining Literacy and Numeracy Achievement Objectives are available.
