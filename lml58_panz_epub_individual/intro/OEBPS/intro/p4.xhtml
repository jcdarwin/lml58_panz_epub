New Zealand has a history of high student achievement and consistently ranks in the top group of countries in international studies. Building on the work of internationally renowned educators such as Sylvia Ashton Warner and Dame Marie Clay and employing the talents of internationally renowned authors such as Joy Cowley and Margaret Mahy, New Zealand has developed a successful educational publishing export business. Our publishers offer resources across the curriculum, from early childhood to tertiary, for learners and teachers.

Freshness, innovation, and a strong grounding in what works in the classroom characterises New Zealand educational publishing. A unique combination of engaging, high-interest material developed by passionate educator-authors, utilising education research and presented using design and layout that helps the learner is at the heart of our work. We tap into the best of what practising teachers do and make this accessible to educators around the world.

New Zealand has been an early adopter of digital technology – to communicate with the rest of the world and to find ways that digital resources can support education.

We are a creative country, famous for our world-class film, media, and game development industries. We are future-focused, able to respond to new ideas and to deliver our resources to users in many different formats.
