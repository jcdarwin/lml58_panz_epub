About us

We exist to provide teachers and students with world-leading, engaging resources. We have developed a unique digital interface as a ‘tool for learning’ for students aged from 8–98+.

Our school series, CSI Literacy, co-published with Pacific Learning, USA, is already distributed in the USA, Australia, New Zealand, Singapore and Abu Dhabi. CSI Literacy is backed by independent data gathered from schools that shows acceleration in literacy achievement for students.

Our contract division, Lift Education, provides educational and publishing strategy, research, concept development, production, distribution and evaluation services to organisations in New Zealand and the USA. Lift also develops training materials for tertiary and adult education.

CSI Literacy product range

CSI – Comprehension Strategies Instruction

CSI – Comprehension Strategies Instruction is an innovative literacy resource for ages 8–13+. Developed across six year levels, CSI kits contain 480 short texts: 240 digital and 240 student book texts with supporting audio. The kits support teachers to scaffold and model through explicit instruction, and allow all students to focus on one digital text, as well as have students interact and reflect on content-area texts.
