Key Links Reading provides teachers with explicit guidance on every page in every book. It’s a complete toolbox for the teaching of shared and guided reading, from emergent through to fluency levels. Enjoy the musical, magical, rhythmical shared reading experience for Years 1 – 4, the levelled text for guided reading and their digital ebook versions.

Into Connectors and Connectors are small group independent books based on the reciprocal reading approach. They encourage students to work in a group with their peers without the teacher. Students working together to unlock text and to think about and discuss what they have read is a powerful learning experience. Explore our wide variety of topics for yourself.

Key Links Writing is a comprehensive resource for the foundation and early years of writing development. It gives teachers a blueprint to successfully implement a writing programme that assists students as they begin their exciting writing journey.
