The product

Print and Digital

AWARD Reading and Sunshine Books have over 2,600 titles in print and on the backlist, over 300 animated, interactive texts and 1000 skill activities online, a television series, and multiple websites offering educational resources, both free and with subscription.

In the United States, AWARD Reading is making submissions for state adoptions to provide supplemental/intervention digital learning programs. The company has been successful in six states so far with more pending.

The Market

AWARD Reading is now embarking on a partnership with a telco in Korea to provide content for English language learning centres. These will form a major learning platform for China, Korea, Japan, Vietnam and Turkey.

In partnership with a software developer, AWARD Reading is working on an app to provide an English language learning course for mobile devices.

With these new developments, millions of children over the next year will learn to read on product written and designed by New Zealanders.

Licensing and opportunities

English Learning programs are the focus for AWARD Reading with rights available for Europe and a selection of other countries. Delivered in a new digital format, these programs feature hundreds of stories, tests and learning activities.
