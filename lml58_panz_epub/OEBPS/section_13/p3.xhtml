The Anger Toolbox

A ground breaking, engaging handbook to assist parents, teachers and other professionals to help children or teens in their care who have difficulty managing their anger.

  • Provides very practical, honest information and strategies for positive change
  • Dip in and out of it for ideas and support
  • Provides anger management skills to teach children and teens – and to use personally
  • Many ideas and choices offered, for every age
  • Nonthreatening
  • Culturally inclusive
  • Colourful and engaging, with rich graphics
  • Includes comments by experts, parents and teachers
  • Launched and endorsed by New Zealand’s Commissioner for Children, Dr, Russell Wills.

Children’s Support Booklets
(ten titles)

For children 8-12 years who are facing difficult life issues.

  • offer key information and strategies to help children cope and build resilience
  • innovative, colourful, hand-sized booklets
  • sensitively written using experts and best evidence
  • full of practical, down-to-earth tips
  • used for individuals, classroom groups, counselling conversations or taken home to refer to
  • Topics include: anger management, bullying, safety and sexual abuse prevention, bereavement, anxiety, family issues including family break up, blended families, addiction, mental illness, illness and injury.
  • Widely used by schools and counsellors, and children’s workers.
