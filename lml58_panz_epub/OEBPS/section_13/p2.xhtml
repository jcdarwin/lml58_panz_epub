Something Has Happened

A proven activity book for young children facing a difficult or traumatic life situation:

  • Helps children find words and courage to process difficult thoughts and feelings
  • Each child personalises their own copy
  • Illustrations for colouring in and interactive activities
  • Successfully used in aftermath of trauma by Australian and New Zealand governments and schools
  • Helpful notes throughout for adults

The Journey Through

An innovative, magazine-style youth handbook assisting teens through difficult life situations:

  • Colourful, engaging graphics
  • Features excellent, evidenced-based information
  • Can be ‘snacked on’
  • Uses youth voices
  • Resiliency building
  • Successfully used in aftermath of trauma by Australian and New Zealand governments and schools.
