Taking New Zealand’s Innovation to the World

Books and digital resources continue to be successful in highlighting the importance of New Zealand writers, developers and educational methods. The Wendy Pye Group has professional development teams that work with teachers throughout the world to support print and technology in the classroom. The group has published in 15 languages and also focuses on the teaching of English as a foreign language.

The publisher, Wendy Pye, was awarded the MBE for her international export success. She is the only publisher to be inducted into the Business Hall of Fame for her work in exporting publishing and education throughout the world. She has also been recognised for her work in helping the educational success of women and girls in developing countries using New Zealand’s educational methods.

Sunshine Books worked with the Wright Group in the United States to introduce shared and guided reading methods and Big Books that New Zealand had so successfully pioneered.

The group also introduced shared and guided reading into South Africa, joining with an NGO to work in partnership with the government on a major drive for literacy. Today this work continues with publication in Zulu, English and a variety of other African languages as well as digital content.
