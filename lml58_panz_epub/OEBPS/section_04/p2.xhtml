Who we are

ESA Publications (NZ) Ltd is a privately owned New Zealand educational publisher with over 20 years’ experience.

Our titles, currently numbering 150, are well known in New Zealand schools, particularly secondary schools. We are the main supplier of books for home study.

What we do

Working with over two hundred practising teachers, some the best in the country, we publish titles to align with New Zealand’s curriculum and assessment standards (NCEA – the National Certificate of Educational Achievement). We update our titles regularly.

We can use this experience to adapt our resources for the curriculum and assessment standards in your country.
