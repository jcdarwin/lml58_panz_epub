"Hello, I’m Jill Eggleton, International Educational Consultant in Literacy, author of over 800 books with a wide variety of teaching experiences spanning over forty years. My cornerstone belief is that students need to be engaged in the texts that they are reading. They need texts that motivate, excite and enrich – humorous, thought provoking modern material, both fiction and non-fiction. We have a responsibility to provide students with material that will set the wheels in motion for a life-long love of reading. GES resources cover shared, guided and independent reading as well as digital and writing development."
