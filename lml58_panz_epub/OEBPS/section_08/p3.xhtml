who we are

Founded by Rhonda Kite and derived from her background in audio production for film and television, Kiwa Media are based in Auckland, New Zealand, with offices in the United States, China and United Arab Emirates.

Our products range from interactive textbooks to multi-lingual children’s stories to resources preserving indigenous languages. They are being used in the continental United States, Alaska, Singapore, Brazil, Australia, China and New Zealand.

our services

Kiwa Media specialises in digital media application development for mobile devices that feature custom audio, narrators, and highly interactive books, which captivate and educate children and adults.

BookSync is an audio book enhanced by synchronized highlighted text that allows readers to relax and be read to, or not, as they follow the text. BookSync™ has been specifically created for adults and applied to novels and non-fiction.

our products

QBook is Kiwa Media’s interactive, multi-functional, multi-lingual flagship product. Our goal with QBook™ is to make reading fun and easy, and promote literacy, spelling and comprehension skills for children and young adults. In addition QBook™ offers multi-lingual features that allow the reader to change the written and narrator language, for example English to Mandarin, or Arabic to Spanish.
