About us

Kiwa Media creates interactive, multi-lingual digital books for the iPad, iPhone, Android and other mobile devices.

what we do

Kiwa Media is a digital media company specialising in accessible learning technologies. Our clients are publishers of children’s books, who desire more than a straight rendition of text. We offer high quality audio, design and interactive features, making reading and learning entertaining without compromising the educational content of the books.

sign language

Kiwa Media are the first company to launch children’s books using AUSLAN, the sign language of Australia. We continue to work with publishers and institutions to develop digital books with a range of features that will support everyone, no matter what their learning difficulties might be.
