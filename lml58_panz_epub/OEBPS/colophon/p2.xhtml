Readium was amended slightly to provide extra functionality, including:

  • reactivation of zoom (in Chrome)
  • redirection to chromeframe for Internet Explorer users
  • navigation from the end of one publication (chapter) to the next
  • detection of the viewport meta tag in Firefox not reliant on getElementsByName (which prevented the page from loading)
  • retain visibility of previous and next navigation buttons even when the toolbar is hidden
  • ensuring that the content extends over the toolbar area when the toolbar is hidden.

As Readium has been developed using Chrome, this publication will look best on Google Chrome, though performance should be reasonable on other major browsers (with the exception of Internet Explorer).
