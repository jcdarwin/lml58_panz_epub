Readium was amended slightly to provide extra functionality, including:

  • reactivation of zoom
  • redirection to chromeframe for Internet Explorer users
  • navigation between the end of one section and the start of the next
  • support for viewing in Safari on the iPad
  • detection of the viewport meta-tag in Firefox not reliant on getElementsByName (which prevented the page from loading)
  • inclusion of the previous and next navigation buttons even when the toolbar is hidden
  • ensuring that the content extends over the toolbar area when the toolbar is hidden.

As Readium has been developed using Google Chrome, this publication will look best in the Chrome browser, though performance should be reasonable in recent versions of other major browsers (with the exception of Internet Explorer).
