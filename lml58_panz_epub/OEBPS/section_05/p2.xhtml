What we do

At Essential Resources we produce a wide range of high-quality classroom resources and ebooks.

Working within the context of New Zealand’s first-class education system we tap into the expertise of highly motivated, creative teachers; translating their successes into leading edge, practical resources for classroom teachers, everywhere.

Innovation inspires us and so, building on the success of our curriculum planning resources, we created iUgo – a digital curriculum planning solution.

Our team of experienced teacher-authors, designers, editors and IT experts is committed to producing best-quality products and services that meet the demands of the 21st century classroom.
