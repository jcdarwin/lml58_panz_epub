Our publications

We produce a wide range of classroom resources, plus teacher support and professional practice books for primary and secondary schools.

These include curriculum-specific and cross curricular publications on topics like English, maths, science, sustainability, ICT, SOLO Taxonomy, special needs, behaviour management
and others.

Our resources are noted in many schools throughout the United Kingdom, Ireland, New Zealand and Australia for their professionalism, relevance and style.

Our ongoing success reflects our ability to continually produce market-specific supplementary educational material that both supports teachers and inspires students.

Curriculum planning

iUgo is our online curriculum planning solution. This flexible digital framework is a ‘click and build’ model that can be tailored to any curriculum. Being cloud-based, it promotes collaboration and enables teachers to create lesson plans that can be saved, changed, shared and accessed anywhere, anytime.
