What we do

Clean Slate Press is a publisher, a packager, and a design house for educational publishers. We combine many years of knowledge and experience with a focus on child-centred learning and a passion for innovative product.

Who we Are

Clean Slate Press is a 100% New Zealand owned and operated company. We publish high-quality educational resources, both for New Zealand and the international market.

We recognize the importance of providing teachers and students with product that is specifically designed to meet the achievement objectives of the market in which it is sold.

Clean Slate Press is proud to be the exclusive educational publisher of one of New Zealand’s most well‑known and awarded children’s writers – Joy Cowley. With Joy, we work to raise the standard of early literacy around the world.

Clean Slate Press has published for a number of prestigious North American customers, including Scholastic Inc., Scholastic Canada, Houghton Mifflin Harcourt, Hameray Publishing, Nelson Canada, and Pearson Canada.

We also sell to ministries of education and other markets in the U.K., Australia, Hong Kong, China, Singapore, Korea, Thailand, and Malaysia.
