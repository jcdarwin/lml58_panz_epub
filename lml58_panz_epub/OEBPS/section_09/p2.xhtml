Who we are

Learning Media is a global multimedia and multicultural learning services company.

We work with publishers, governments, and NGOs worldwide to develop and distribute learning resources and programmes, focused on effective teaching and learning, that are tailored to the needs and dynamics of local markets and cultures.

Our award-winning team of experts has extensive experience in curriculum and professional development, educational consultancy, educational and children’s publishing, digital content integration, health literacy, and adult education.

Our resources have been an integral part of New Zealand’s successful education system for more than 100 years. They are underpinned by a strong research base and an in-depth understanding of instructional design.
