Product Range

An extensive range of world-class learning resources from early childhood to Grade 8 is available for distribution, adaptation, and translation.

Visit our website for the full range.

Our resources include:

  • levelled student texts from emergent reader through to chapter books
  • fiction and non-fiction texts in a variety of text types
  • audio support
  • teachers’ notes giving guidance on teaching strategies, lesson plans, and follow-up activities
  • combined print and digital resources to provide the best learning opportunities for students
  • total classroom solutions offering whole-class, small-group, and independent instruction.

For teachers we provide:

  • teachers’ notes for all series
  • planning and assessment tools
  • tools to support students’ writing and reading
  • professional texts, outlining the best of New Zealand educational practice, ready for use in classrooms around the world.

Services

We provide concept development services, full publishing services in all media, and professional development design and delivery. Our expertise includes literacy, mathematics, science, and health and ranges from early childhood to adult literacy. Our satisfied clients value our collaborative and solution-focused approach.
