New Zealand has a world-leading education system, and our vibrant educational publishers play an important role in our learners’ success. Educational resources produced by New Zealanders are sought after, and are making a difference to the lives of students and teachers throughout the world.

The companies in this book represent the breadth, depth, and diversity of New Zealand educational publishers. From smaller, specialised companies to larger companies providing resources across a range of subjects and for a range of ages, we all share a passion for education and the success of learners.

Take this journey through New Zealand’s learning landscape to find publishers who can contribute to learners’ success in your market.
